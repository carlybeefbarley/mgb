ABOUT US

With MyGameBuilder you can learn to code with the help of our tutorials and mentors and start building games all within the browser.

MYGAMEBUILDER

MyGameBuilder (2007) was originally a flash-based web application that allowed users to easily build games all within the browser. It had a sizable, active community of users that strove to become better game builders. The new MyGameBuilder goes beyond and focuses on creating a learning environment for coding, while improving on the engagement of the community.

THE TEAM

Our team is made up of experienced developers with a passion for learning and teaching. We believe the best way to learn is by doing, and MyGameBuilder is the perfect platform for doers. We want to share the joy of coding through engaging activities and stimulating exercises.

THE FOUNDER

David Golds is the founder of MyGameBuilder. The original MyGameBuilder started as a hobby project and grew to become much more through the experince David gained from senior positions at Microsoft and VMWare.
