FAQ

What will be taught in class?

We will be teaching fundamental programming concepts needed to build games using JavaScript and Phaser. Students will get a hands-on learning experience of the basics of JavaScript from variables to loop and functions then apply what they learned to build simple games with Phaser, an HTML5 game framework.

Do students need prior programming experience?

Our curriculum is formed for students with no prior programming experience. This does not necessarily mean students with prior experience are discouraged. Visual programming tools like Scratch are commonly used to make games, but do not teach coding which is the big step forward that we offer.

What will the class be like?

Students will be using MyGameBuilder in class and will follow along with the instructors. We will have two instructors available for one-on-ones with every student for any additional help that is needed. Students are expected to bring their own laptops.

Where will the classes be?

The classes will be held on the 4th Floor at Lincoln Square South Tower in Bellevue Way NE and NE 4th St.
